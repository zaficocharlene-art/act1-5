Activity 2 
Drink Tracker is a system designed to help users record and monitor their daily beverage consumption. It allows users to track the amount of water and other drinks they consume throughout the day, helping them maintain proper hydration and develop healthier drinking habits. The system can also provide summaries of daily intake, making it easier for users to monitor their progress and achieve their hydration goals.
